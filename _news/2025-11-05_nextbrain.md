I had the honor to contribute to Eugenio's masterpiece 
["A probabilistic histological atlas of the human brain for MRI segmentation"](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-025-09708-2),
which introduces the NextBrain atlas and was published in Nature.

Note that it now comes with a faster segmentation algorithm, lead by Oula and available in preprint: 
["Fast segmentation with the NextBrain histological atlas"](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2025.09.22.673638v1)
